BYANIHAN

Byanihan is a co-living community grown out of our pilot site in Siargao Island, Philippines. Inspired by the culture of the island, we break down the components of a single 'home' into a variety of well-designed, private dwelling quarters arranged around communal spaces. Situated on a hilltop embraced by stunning sea and forest views, inhabitants together reimagine shared needs and activities by living with nature and one another.
