BYANIHAN

Byanihan is membership community development on a hilltop property in Siargao Island, Philippines. Inspired by the culture of the island, we created a single ‘home’ spread out on a landscape embraced by stunning sea and forest views. Reimagining which needs, activities, and experiences are shared among inhabitants, Byanihan breaks down the components of a 'home' into a variety of well-designed, private dwelling quarters arranged around communal spaces.
